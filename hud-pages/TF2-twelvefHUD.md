##### A HUD with an odd vision with quite a familiar style of the original interface.
*This HUD is still work in progress! Expect bugs, placeholders and unfinished aspects of the HUD.*

This HUD is being made just for me to match my vision for a TF styled HUD to enjoy playing Casual and Competitive 6s.
As for now it is playable and the main gameplay aspects are mostly done. Customizations are accessible but might break things or not switch stuff at all.
I might update the HUD for other resolutions than 16:9 in the future. I won't support DX8, but it should be completely fine with *maybe* minor visual glitches.
